The Problem

Your health history is everywhere and nowhere.

Right now, your medical records live across WhatsApp forwards, Gmail attachments, a crumpled folder at home, and three lab portals you forgot the password to.

When you see a new specialist or renew health insurance, your history gets guessed — or ignored. In a high-stakes scenario, that gap costs you.

How It Works

From scattered records to a living passport.

01
Create Your Health Identity

Link your Ayushman Bharat Health Account (ABHA) to activate your PreventID. Your verified digital health ID becomes the anchor for all future records.

02
Connect Your Data Sources

Authorize connected labs, clinics, and wearables. PreventID automatically aggregates historical and ongoing data — from SRL and Thyrocare to Apollo and your fitness tracker.

03
Get Intelligent Alerts

The system builds your risk profile and flags overdue screenings aligned to ICMR guidelines. Know exactly what you need — cardiac markers at 40, colonoscopy at 45 — before your doctor has to ask.

04
Share. Confidently.

At any touchpoint — new hospital, insurance renewal, or corporate wellness program — generate a verified summary link. One tap. Structured data. Trusted by providers.
